How it works

One script tag.
Instant savings.

XOXO sits at the edge of your infrastructure. It classifies every request, routes bots to lightweight cached responses, and your origin server never has to do the work.

01
Every request is classified
XOXO identifies whether each visitor is a human, a retrieval agent, or a training scraper — using behavioural signals, not just user-agent strings.
02
Bots get a cached response
AI agents get a lightweight structured response served from edge cache. Your origin server is never touched. No CPU. No bandwidth. No bill.
03
An llms.txt is auto-generated
A single machine-readable file gives agents what they need in one pass — stopping them from crawling 5,000 pages to find it themselves.
04
You see exactly what you saved
A live dashboard shows origin calls avoided, bandwidth saved, and the exact dollar amount XOXO saved you this month. Every month.
